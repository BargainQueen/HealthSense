##### \# HealthSense

##### 

HealthSense is an AI-powered medical assistant that helps users understand their health symptoms and medical reports using artificial intelligence. The goal of this project is to make basic health guidance accessible, simple, and understandable for everyone.



###### \## Features



\- **Symptom Analysis** – Users can enter symptoms and receive AI-based health insights.

\- **Voice Symptom Input** – Speak symptoms using voice input.

\- **AI Risk Level Indicator** – Shows health risk levels (Low, Moderate, High).

\- **Medicine Safety Check** – Provides general safety guidance for medicines.

\- **Medical Report Explainer** – Upload lab reports and get simple explanations.

\- **Nearby Hospital Finder** – Helps users locate nearby hospitals quickly.

\- **Multi-language Explanation** – Supports English, Tamil, and Hindi explanations.



⚠️ This tool provides informational guidance only and is **not a replacement for professional medical advice**.

Many people struggle to understand medical symptoms and reports. HealthSense was built to simplify health information using AI so that users can quickly understand possible health conditions and decide when to seek professional medical help.
